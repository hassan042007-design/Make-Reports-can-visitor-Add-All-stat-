🐠 Environmental Responsibility

Each participant is to provide his own insurance. Respect and protect the Coral Reef and Marine life. Do not touch or collect live or dead animal or shell either from the sea or the beach.

✅ By Acknowledging This, You Declare That You:

  • Recently underwent a medical examination and were found fit for diving (a medical statement is mandatory).
  • Do not suffer from any chronic disease (such as diseases concerning the blood circulatory system or respiratory system).
  • Will plan your dives within the no-decompression limit according to the dive table or diving computer.
  • Will dive at a depth that does not exceed the recommended depth-limit with respect to your diving experience, under no circumstances exceeding 40m.
  • Will obey the orders of the diving instructor or base manager.
  • Will never dive alone.

🤝 Buddy Diving

There is no better security measure than to dive together with an experienced and reliable partner. Prior to diving, discuss the planned diving excursion with your partner and inform yourself about his/her diving experience. Check the most important hand-signals. Before entering the water perform a thorough buddy-check and during the dive do not lose sight of your partner.

⚠️ Additional Security Rules

  • Dive within your limitations (experience and physical condition).
  • Constantly monitor your instruments.
  • Alternative air source, buoyancy control device, depth gauge and/or computer and tables are mandatory.
  • Make sure that your equipment is well maintained and working properly. Check it prior to every dive.
  • Only dive while being in good physical condition and never under the influence of medicine, alcohol or drugs.
  • Before diving, make sure you know all peculiarities of the dive-site.
  • A repetitive dive may bear an increased risk of becoming a decompression dive. Plan repetitive dives so that each successive dive is at a shallower depth.
  • After repetitive dive stop diving 24 hours prior to flying.

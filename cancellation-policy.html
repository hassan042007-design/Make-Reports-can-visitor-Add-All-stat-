Cancellation and No-Show Policy for Excursions

We Value Your Time

🌟 Our Commitment

Red Sea Diving Safari is committed to providing exceptional service in a timely manner. Your diving experience and satisfaction is very important to us. In order to maintain the quality of service, we need you to be on time and adhere to all time schedules set forth by the diving center.

📋 Cancellation Policy

Please be advised that due to limited number of seating's, high demand and respect for other guests, any cancellation for a reserved excursion, after Diving Center office hours, will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

🕐 Diving Center office hours are from 9am – 5pm daily. Cancellations during office hours will not be subject to any cancellation fee.
⚠️ Any No Show for a reserved excursion will also be subject to a 50% No show/cancellation fee.

⏱️ Be Prepared

Please be on time. Prepare your equipment early, so you are ready when the boat leaves.
